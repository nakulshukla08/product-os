## Prioritization Framework

We use **ICE scoring** (1–10 scale):

- **Impact** — How much will this move the needle?
- **Confidence** — How sure are we about impact?
- **Ease** — How easy is it to ship?

Score = (Impact + Confidence + Ease) / 3. Higher = higher priority.
