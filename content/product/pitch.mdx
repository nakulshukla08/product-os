# Pitch

## One-Liner

Acme Platform reduces time-to-production for development teams by 40% through unified tooling and intelligent automation.

## Problem

Teams spend weeks stitching together disparate tools—CI/CD, monitoring, API management—leading to context switching and integration debt.

## Solution

A single platform that integrates the full development lifecycle with a consistent UX and shared data model.

## Traction

- 50+ early adopters
- 3x MoM growth in active projects
- NPS 72
