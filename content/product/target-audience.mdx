# Target Audience

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

- **Company size:** 10–200 engineers
- **Stage:** Series A–C, product-market fit achieved
- **Stack:** Modern (Kubernetes, microservices, APIs)
- **Pain:** Tool sprawl, integration complexity, slow deployments

## Personas

### Dev Lead (Primary)
- Owns delivery velocity and developer experience
- Frustrated by fragmented tooling
- Values simplicity and speed

### Platform Engineer (Secondary)
- Maintains internal tooling and infra
- Wants fewer systems to operate
- Cares about extensibility
